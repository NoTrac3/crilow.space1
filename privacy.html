Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2025

1. Data Collection

For security purposes, we may collect and process:

  • IP addresses (with user consent via cookie banner)
  • Discord user IDs when using our bots
  • Server IDs where our bots are installed
  • Moderation actions taken through our bots

2. Data Usage

Collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Preventing abuse and malicious activity
  • Improving bot functionality
  • Complying with legal requests

3. Data Retention

We retain logs for 30 days unless required for ongoing investigations. Banned user data is retained indefinitely for security purposes.

4. Third Parties

We do not sell data. Limited information may be shared with:

  • Discord for abuse reporting
  • Law enforcement when legally compelled
  • Partnered vigilante networks (anonymous threat data only)

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR/CCPA, you may request:

  • Access to your stored data
  • Deletion of non-essential data
  • Correction of inaccurate information

Submit requests via our contact page.
